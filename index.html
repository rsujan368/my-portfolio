My Story

From Supermarket Floors
to Search Engine Rankings

My journey into digital marketing didn't start with a textbook — it started behind the counters of a supermarket. Working at Kathmandu Supermarket, I handled marketing campaigns, supplier negotiations, and in-store promotions. That hands-on hustle sparked a curiosity about the digital world.

I began self-learning digital marketing, and SEO pulled me in completely. There was no turning back. After internships at Global IOT Nepal and Green Computing Nepal, I joined Codavatar Tech where I had the privilege of working on KrispCall , a globally recognized product driving measurable organic traffic growth.

Today at Meta Cloud Solutions, I lead SEO strategy for products like quickconnect.biz and danfe.io, while also mentoring team members and collaborating with content creators to shape social media marketing strategies across platforms.
